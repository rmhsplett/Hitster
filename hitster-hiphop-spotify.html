How to Play
  1. Draw a card — the song stays hidden from everyone.
  2. Tap Play — a 30-second Spotify preview plays right in the card.
  3. Everyone listens and guesses the song and the year it dropped.
  4. The active player taps a slot in their timeline where they think it belongs.
  5. Tap Reveal — correct song + correct slot = keep the card!
  6. First to collect the target cards wins!
